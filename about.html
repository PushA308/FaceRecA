What is FaceRecA ?






Need ?

Maintaining the attendance is essential in every one of the foundations for checking the performance of students. Manual entering of attendance in logbooks becomes a difficult task and it also wastes the time.

Solution ?

FaceRecA is one of the advance methodology used to mark attendace of the students.The presence of each student will be updated in a database. FaceRecA gives much more solutions with accurate results in user interactive manner rather than existing attendance systems.

What it does ?

Smart Attendance using Real Time Face Recognition is a real world solution which comes with day to day activities of handling students.Our system maintains the attendance records of students automatically.The results showed improved performance over manual attendance management system.
